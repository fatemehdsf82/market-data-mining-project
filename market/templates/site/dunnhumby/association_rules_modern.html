Understanding Association Rules

Association rule mining helps identify relationships between different items purchased together, enabling better product recommendations and inventory planning.

Support

Measures how frequently the itemset appears in the dataset. Higher support means the combination is more common.

Confidence

Indicates the reliability of the rule. Shows the likelihood of buying the consequent when the antecedent is purchased.

Lift

Measures the strength of association. Lift > 1 indicates positive correlation, < 1 indicates negative correlation.

Applications

Product recommendations, cross-selling strategies, store layout optimization, and promotional bundling.
