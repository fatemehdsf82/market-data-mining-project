Understanding Association Rules

Support

How frequently the itemset appears in the dataset (0-1)

Confidence

How often rule is correct (likelihood of consequent when antecedent is present)

Lift

How much more likely the consequent is when antecedent is present

  • Lift > 1: Positive correlation
  • Lift = 1: Independence
  • Lift < 1: Negative correlation
