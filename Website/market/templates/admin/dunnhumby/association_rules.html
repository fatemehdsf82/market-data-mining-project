About Association Rules Mining

Apriori Algorithm

Uses frequent itemset mining to discover relationships between products in shopping baskets. Identifies patterns like "customers who buy X also buy Y".

Key Metrics

  • Support: How frequently items appear together
  • Confidence: Likelihood of consequent given antecedent
  • Lift: Strength of association (>1 = positive)

Business Applications

  • Product recommendations
  • Store layout optimization
  • Cross-selling strategies
  • Inventory management
